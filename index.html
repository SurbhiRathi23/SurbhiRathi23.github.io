ABOUT US

SMH Hospital is a non-profit worldwide leader
in medical care for everyone who needs healing.

surgical sub-specialties within a single institution, empowering

needs and treat the whole person.

and technology that reflects why Mayo Clinic is the trusted leader in healthcare worldwide.
